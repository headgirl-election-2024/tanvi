My vision

The perspective I hold for DMC is to foster a vibrant community where every student feels valued and empowered. I aim to promote inclusivity, academic excellence, and personal growth. Through collaboration and innovation, we will create a supportive environment that encourages leadership, creativity, and lifelong learning. Together, let's make our school a place where everyone thrives. My campaign includes the following initiatives, which I believe will significantly enhance DMC:

  • Firstly, I'd like to re-open clubs such as the Book Club and the Journalism Club to promote reading and writing. I’d also like to bring back the eco-life club to promote sustainability.

  • As head girl, I’d also encourage outdoor learning. (For ex: During activity periods. I’ll explain in detail during my face-to-face campaign.)

  • I also plan on doing a lot of interclass competitions and activities such as interclass debates, chess and art competitions, and other sports activities.

  • Reviving the Sports Club will lead to the recruitment of new members, and activities such as football, badminton, basketball, and volleyball tournaments will also take place for sports lovers!

  • I’ve been told that there’s a lack of gym and sports equipment at school. I’ll try my best to introduce new gears for y’all to train.

  • I’d ensure that our school attends talks (for ex: Career Guidance, psychological well-being, communication skills enhancement, etc…)

  • I believe that communication is the key to improvement and therefore I’d conduct regular meetings with classes to get feedback and y’all’s opinions.

  • I’m all ears for new ideas about improvements that can be made to the school or any ideas or designs you have in mind for the school jackets and uniforms.

  • I’d also organize some “out–of–school” fundraising activities (that is, activities done out of the school compound to raise money and awareness. Details will be communicated during my face-to-face campaign)

  • I believe that each student of DMC has the right to express his opinion and therefore I’ll put a link on the official Insta page of DMC where any anonymous communication could be done via student to the head team.

Cast your vote wisely! Your support means a lot.
